About

Though often commissioned for my indeterminate, universally appealing sound I’ve continuously been the voice of major campaigns in the Caribbean, and am currently the IVR of the region’s largest telecoms provider.

Between life in the UK and the Middle East, I developed a love for playing characters which I continue to pursue based in Canada. Yea, I get around.

Remote recording and Source Connect/phone patch capable.
